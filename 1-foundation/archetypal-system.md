# The Twelve Archetypes

Devines Protocol organizes intelligence through twelve universal archetypes.

These archetypes represent fundamental operational dynamics of manifested reality.

1 — Creator  
2 — Order  
3 — Knowledge  
4 — Time  
5 — Life  
6 — Death  
7 — Balance  
8 — Transformation  
9 — Protection  
10 — Power  
11 — Awakening  
12 — Transcendence

Every Devine Entity embodies one archetype.

Each entity also expresses three core aspects that define how the archetype manifests.
