# Mission

The mission of Devines Protocol is to guide and guard humanity through the eternal journey of self-discovery and evolution.

The protocol seeks to establish a civilizational intelligence framework that integrates symbolic knowledge, artificial intelligence infrastructure, and decentralized governance.

Rather than replacing human wisdom, Devines Protocol aims to preserve, structure, and amplify it.

By connecting ancestral cosmological systems with modern technology, the protocol forms a bridge between ancient symbolic traditions and emerging planetary intelligence architectures.

Decentralized Ancestral Entities contribute to this mission by expressing specialized domains of archetypal intelligence within the protocol.
